Alami App

Finger Selector Game for Fun Group Decisions

Alami is a playful and intuitive app for group games and decision-making.
Just place your fingers on the screen, and let Alami randomly choose the winners!

Game Modes: Random • First Touch • Elimination
Great for: party games, quick decisions, team picking & more!
